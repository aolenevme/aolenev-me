Russia is a software leader. It produced great number of ecosystems in recent years: Sber, Yandex, VK, Ozon, Tinkoff, Avito. Nobody did it so well except USA and China.

But the country is imposed sanctions now. The hardest ones in the world.

The questions is how can the IT sector can be recovered when yet another Oracle, SAP, or Windows says bye-bye to the russian market every week? The whole world contributed to this software for the 40 years. And it is impossible to rewrite all the software having such a small labor market.

To my mind, there is only one way: Open Source. Don`t reinvent OracleDB! Become an expert in Postgres! In all other cases, it will be the waste of money.
