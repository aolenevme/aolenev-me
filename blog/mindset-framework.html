Mindset Framework

June 9, 2022

The main purpose of code quality investments is cultivating of a "mindset framework" in a growing team.

Developers must be restricted: where to put files, how to name functions, what features of a language to use and etc.

What is allowed - and what is not.

As a result, we get fast and consistent time-to-market because extra development rants are avoided and you are hedged by increasing bus factor.
